    What is NVDA?

    NonVisual Desktop Access (NVDA) is a free and open-source, portable screen reader for Microsoft Windows. Providing feedback via synthetic speech and Braille, it enables blind or vision impaired people to access computers running Windows for no more cost than a sighted person. NVDA allows blind and vision impaired people to access and interact with the Windows operating system and many third-party applications. NVDA is developed by NV Access, with contributions from the community.
